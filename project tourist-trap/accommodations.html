A MORE CHEAPER OPTION

Using Airbnb can be a cheaper alternative to traditional hotels because it often offers more affordable options, such as private rooms, shared spaces, or entire homes, which can be less expensive than booking a hotel room. Additionally, Airbnb hosts may provide discounts for longer stays, and guests can save money by cooking meals in fully equipped kitchens rather than dining out. Airbnb also allows travelers to choose accommodations that fit their budget and preferences, often in local neighborhoods, which can offer a more authentic experience at a lower price point.

WHY YOU SHOULD RENT A CAR

Renting a car in Curaçao is a great choice for holidaymakers as it offers flexibility and convenience to explore the island at your own pace. Public transportation options are limited, so having a rental car allows you to visit remote beaches, hidden attractions, and local hotspots without depending on schedules. It also makes it easier to travel comfortably with your belongings, and you can enjoy the freedom to explore both the popular destinations and off-the-beaten-path areas.
